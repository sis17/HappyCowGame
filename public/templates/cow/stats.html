Welfare is affected by extreme body condition or rumen PH levels. As muck and oligos intake increases, welfare suffers. Disiease and card actions also affect welfare.

The effects of welfare:

  • 5 - +1pt per ration absorbed
  • 4 - +1pt per ration absorbed
  • 3 - +1pt per ration absorbed
  • ...
  • -3 - cow eats -1 ration, -1pt per ration absorbed
  • -4 - cow eats -1 ration, -1pt per ration absorbed
  • -5 - cow doesn't eat, -1pt per ingredient absorbed
  • -6 - cow doesn't eat, -1pt per ingredient absorbed
  • -7 - cow dies

Body condition shows how fat the cow is. It is affected by different events, and impacts the welfare of the cow heavily. A very fat, or very lean cow will suffer welfare loss.

The effects of body condition:

  • 3 - -1 welfare per turn
  • 2 - +1 welfare per turn
  • 1
  • 0
  • -1
  • -2 - -1 welfare per turn
  • -3 - -1 welfare per turn
  • -4 - cow dies

Every ration that is not absorbed as milk or meat, and passes out of the cow becomes muck. As muck builds up it reduces the cow's welfare.

The effects of muck are:

  • 1
  • 2
  • 3 - -1 welfare (once)
  • 4
  • 5 and above - -1 welfare (once)

Oligos is a nutrition boost for the cow, but too much will become useless and even have a negative effect on the cow's welfare. Every time and Oligos ingreident is absorbed, the marker increases.

The effects of each oligos absorbed are:

  • 1 - scores 10 pts
  • 2 - scores 2 pts, -1 welfare (once)
  • 3 and above - -1 welfare (once)
